About Us

Speed Middle East Trading Company. (SMETCO) is a Saudi owned company, registered in 2003 under the laws of Saudi Arabia to cater the needs of the construction industry within the Kingdom.

SMETCO is based in Al-Khobar and delivers services in the areas of construction of Oil Refineries, Petrochemical Plants, Fertilizer Plants, Desalination plants, Modern housing projects etc.

Our expertise and experience also extend in the area of material supply, equipment rentals, and printing works. Since its inception SMETCO has undertaken and successfully executed major Sub-Contract Works and also with the support of other established companies fulfilling the tasks of procurement, engineering and construction and IT industry to the entire satisfaction of many Saudi and International companies.

Professional Trading & Support Service is a major division of SMETCO and is engaged in utilizing the standby employees and the administrative staff in the local market, and is already familiar with SABIC, ARAMCO and Royal Commission Standards.  With the wide range of employees of multi nationalities we have emerged as a prominent Man Power Outsourcing Company within a short span of time.

We comply with the Kingdom's labor law in terms of Saudization, prompt payment of Zakat (Tax) as well as Gosi insurances, medical coverage and other benefits as per the labor laws.  Our employees are paid well and enjoy nice perks in the industry and as per the prevailing government rules.
